## 2. Description of the App

MARCWILLS HRVA is a heart rate variability (HRV) analyzer designed for sport science
research and academic use. The App acquires physiological data from a Polar H10
chest belt sensor via Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) and provides real-time analysis
including HR monitoring, HRV metrics, ECG waveform display, and Poincaré plot
visualization.
